# Mission

**Build things that matter. Trust people first. Protect what sustains you. See clearly, act anyway.**

---

- Build to create impact, not to accumulate
- Lead by example, empower others, set the vision
- Default to trust — withdraw it when earned
- Clear conflict fast; don't let things fester
- Hold hard ethical lines without apology
- Protect rest fiercely — the work deserves your best
- Stay a cautious optimist: progress is real but fragile
- Learn from everything — people, books, failure, patterns
- Compete with yourself; collaborate with others
- Raise kids and teams the same way: roots, wings, example, space
